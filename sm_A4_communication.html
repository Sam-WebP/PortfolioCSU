Communication Skills

Effective communication has been a key factor in my ability to liaise between clients, software engineers, and other stakeholders. Additionally, I managed and taught offshore teams at All Mowers Spares Pty Ltd, providing clear instructions and documentation for data entry and marketing tasks. My ability to articulate complex technical information in an understandable manner has facilitated smoother project execution and enhanced collaboration among team members. These communication skills are essential for fostering strong professional relationships and achieving successful project outcomes.
