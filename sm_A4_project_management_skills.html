Project Management Skills

I have experience using various project management tools such as Jira and Trello to track progress, manage tasks, and ensure timely delivery of projects. My ability to manage resources, time, and scope effectively has resulted in the successful implementation of several high-impact projects, meeting both client expectations and company goals.
